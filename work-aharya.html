Aharya: Omni-Channel E-commerce Suite

A unified digital ecosystem seamlessly connecting web, mobile, and in-store operations for a leading fashion retail brand, optimizing customer experience and management efficiency.

E-commerce Web Dev Mobile Apps POS Integration 2024

CrudOps Team

October 26, 2024

The Challenge

Aharya faced fragmentation across its digital channels. Their web, mobile, and physical stores operated on disparate systems, leading to inconsistent data and a disjointed shopping experience. A cohesive, omni-channel solution was imperative.

Our Solution

CrudOps developed an integrated suite that unifies all customer touchpoints. This included a modern e-commerce platform, native mobile apps, and real-time in-store POS synchronization. The goal was to ensure seamless inventory, personalized customer journeys, and efficient management.

Key Results

The unified suite brought significant improvements:

“Within six months, we saw a 35% increase in online sales and our inventory management became flawless.” — Aharya Leadership

Operational efficiency improved by 40%, significantly reducing manual efforts. Customers now enjoy a consistent, frictionless experience across all channels, reinforcing brand loyalty and paving the way for scalable growth.
